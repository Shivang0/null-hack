# Client Portal Guide

This guide explains how to use the NULLHACK Security Consultancy Client Portal to request, track, and receive the results of your security engagement.

---

## 1. Accessing the Portal

### Getting an Invitation

Before you can access the portal you need an invitation from your NULLHACK engagement lead:

1. Your lead sends an invitation to your email address.
2. Check your inbox for an email with subject **You have been invited to NULLHACK**.
3. Click the invitation link (valid for 7 days).
4. If you do not have an account, register with the same email address.
5. The portal dashboard is now accessible at your team-provided URL.

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the portal URL provided in your engagement kick-off email.
2. Enter the email and password you registered with.
3. If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password** on the login page.

---

## 2. Submitting an Engagement Request

The intake form lets you formally request a security engagement.

### Steps

1. From the dashboard, click **New Engagement Request**.
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Client Name** - your organization name
   - **Engagement Type** - choose from: `pentest`, `audit`, `compliance`, or `incident_response`
   - **Scope** - describe the systems, applications, or environments to be assessed
   - **Contact Email** - receives status notifications
3. Click **Submit Request**.
4. The request appears on your dashboard with status **Pending**.

### After Submission

Your engagement lead will review the request and advance it to **Scoping** within one business day.

## 3. Tracking Engagement Progress

Engagement statuses in order: pending, scoping, in_progress, reporting, delivered, closed.

**Open the dashboard** to find your engagement. The current status is shown next to the engagement name. Click the row for detail.

You will receive an email notification on each status change. The Activity tab shows a live event feed.

## 4. Downloading Reports

When your engagement reaches Delivered status, the final report is available for download.

1. Open the dashboard and click on your engagement.
2. Click **View Reports** in the detail panel.
3. Select the report you want (e.g., Executive Summary, Technical Findings).
4. Click **Download** and choose a format: CSV (opens in Excel) or JSON (raw data).

Report sections include: Executive Summary, Technical Findings, Remediation Guidance, and Risk Scoring.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The invitation link expired.**
Contact your engagement lead to request a new invitation.

**Q: I submitted the form but do not see it in my dashboard.**
Refresh the page. If it still does not appear, confirm your workspace access with your lead.

**Q: My report shows no data.**
The report may need to be generated first. Ask your lead to run the generation step.

**Q: Can I share portal access with a colleague?**
Yes. Ask your engagement lead to send a separate invitation to your colleague email address.

**Q: How do I change my contact email?**
Go to Settings > Profile and update your email address.
